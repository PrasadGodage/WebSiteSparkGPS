About Us

Spark GPS System is most trusted name in the vehicle tracking industries.We are working more than 8 years in GPS devices and IOT product software solution.

Spark GPS provide single platform to track , monitor and manage your vehicle , assets, fuel consumption and many more...

Spark always approach Innovation, Inclusivity and collaboration. We are commited to provide services all over maharastra for track your vehicle any time any where.

Last 8 years we are working on government and also private company projects in Waste Management Solution.

VISION

Our exceptional skills in being a fast and flexible partner reflect our needs to be closer to our clients in every world region. Long-standing experience, reliable supply chain, highest technology process models stand for our ability to produce millions of IoT devices to our clients.

MISSION

We aspire to become one of the global leaders of providing unique IoT solutions that contribute to making people's lives easier. We are open-minded to conclude the environment to the creative, ambitious work professional in Lithuania and the rest of the world to grow and contribute towards our Mission.

VALUES

We not only work but also live to help and share kindness to the people, especially to those who need our help the most. We are keeping our values by continuously and courageously creating synergy between Teltonika IoT Group and business partners, as well as clients.
